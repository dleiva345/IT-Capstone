IT Capstone
Automatic Scheduling-Selection

Description of Project

Team selection/assignment in IT Capstone Project has been increasingly challenging due to record high numbers of students and projects in recent semesters. Every student has his/her project/teammate preference. The instructor usually spends hours trying to assign students to projects and to make everyone happy. Even with such efforts, the final solution may not be optimal. Thus, in this project, we are trying to design and implement an algorithm to automate this task and also to reach a near-optimal solution. Eventually, we want to be able to extend this solution to more complex application scenarios. We also plan to make it a web-based application such that more people can use this product.
